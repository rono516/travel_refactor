TERMS AND CONDITIONS OF TOURS OPERATION

1. RESERVATIONS AND PAYMENTS

  1. Reservations are made by contacting WTWS directly, herein after referred to as the “Company”, or through an agent, if your booking is made by one.

  2. Safaris are booked and only confirmed on receipt of a 50% deposit of the basic safari rate. There shall be no binding contract until the deposit has been paid. Failure to remit your deposit on time may result in an automatic release of the confirmed space and the possible inability to reinstate your reservation. When the Company acknowledges receipt in writing of the deposit to you, it shall be deemed to constitute acceptance and confirmation of the reservation. By paying a deposit, you the client and WTWS are deemed to be under contract for the dates quoted for your safari. Deposits are not refundable- the very principle of accepting a deposit being to confirm arrangements, and to mitigate the total loss of a safari booking in the event of a postponement or a cancellation, and to ensure a commitment by both parties.

  3. The balance is due on arrival before commencement of your safari. If the balance is not paid on this date, the Company shall be entitled at its discretion to treat your reservations as cancelled and consequently your deposit will be forfeit.

  4. Any additions to your reservation made within sixty (60) days of commencement of your safari may be accepted- provided space is available, payment in full is received, and documentation finalized before departure.

  5. Bookings are also conditional on you the client signing a RELEASE FORM which must be received by us before your arrival.

2. POSTPONEMENT REQUESTS

  1. Any postponement request of reservation must be in writing. The company at its sole discretion, retains the right either to accept or refuse such postponement request, which shall only be effective upon its acknowledged receipt by the company.

  2. In the event of any agreed postponement, the deposit will be debited only if the booked hotels/lodge in your safari itinerary charges differently according to the tour season at the time. Otherwise deposit/the balance will be put forward to the new dates. In the event of an agreed cancellation, the difference due on the full deposit will be billed and subject to immediate settlement.

  3. If you request a postponement of your safari after the final balance has been paid or due (within 60 days ahead of safari starting date), and in the event the company does not agree to a postponement, the whole basic safari fee is forfeited. If the company, at its sole discretion, does agree to a postponement, the whole basic safari fee is forfeit. If the company, at its sole discretion, does agree to a postponement, the deposit (50 of the basic safari fee) will be forfeit.

3. CANCELLATION AND NO SHOWS

Any cancellation of reservation must be in writing and shall only be honored if signed by the intended traveler or his/her agent if the booking was through agent. The following are the cancellation rules applicable if a written advice of cancellation is received:-

  1. 56 days and above before departure - Refund of 90% of deposit.

  2. 40 days to 56 days before departure – Refund of 65% of deposit.

  3. 26 days to 39 days before departure – Refund of 50% of deposit.

  4. 13 days to 25 days before departure – Refund of 25% of deposit.

Less than 13 days before departure – No refund at all.

4. REFUNDS

No refunds are given (1) if you only complete a portion of your safari booking and leave early. The safari section left unused cannot be refunded or reused on a future safari booking.
